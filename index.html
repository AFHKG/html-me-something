Visit Small Change STL!

Not your typical cocktail bar. Relaxed, cool atmosphere. Wonderful drinks. A must for Tom Waits fans.

Planter's House

Visit Planter's House!

Creatively flavored and beautifully made cocktails. The setting is as pretty as its neighborhood Lafayette.

  • "The cocktails were some of the best I've ever had. I'm giving them 5 stars based on drinks alone-they're seriously that good."
  • "The cocktails are stunning to the eye and the taste buds. Excellent date night environment."
  • "They have so many fantastic seasonal cocktails to will blow your mind. The interior is charmingly historic and they have a wonderful outdoor space."

The Gin Room

Visit The Gin Room!

The best place for gin cocktails. House-made tonics and an unparalleled selection of gin makes this place a destination spot for gin aficionados.

  • "Natasha is amazing and super knowledgeable about her drinks. The cocktail menu is extensive and very exciting for a gin lover."
  • "They're really passionate here about their gin. They know their craft."
  • "There is a great selection of specialty gin. Highly recommended from a former gin hater."
